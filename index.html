GroupChoice

Inspiration

In college my friends and I would go out for dinner every week. However, 7-10 people deciding on one place to go is never easy. Going back and forth between Yelp and WhatsApp to check out the suggestions and see who prefers which option was often quite tedious.

Functionality

Groupchoice combines two apps into one providing a simple interface to create polls and vote on the option you prefer. Built in chat also allows for discussion and comments to be made very easily. You can browse nearby places or search for anything particular like “Sushi” or “Afghan cuisine”.

Tech Stack

Firebase, Google Places API, MapKit and CoreLocation, SnapKit, Cocoapods, Programmatic UI
